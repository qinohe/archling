Welcome.


So you installed archiso and/or made this ISO with it, and it's running, nice!

If you found this as ISO on the internet, it's not mine, you should not trust it as mine, that said

There is a lot of software on this build, I won't go trough them, as I think you know how to find them.

A few configs and scripts are opened up, with Geany, already, so you check or change them for this session.

If you are familiar with I3WM, just take a look at keybinds.txt tab, in Geany, for the extra keybinds.

In the case, you are not in any way familiar with i3WM, or tilling, I suggest to first read the i3WM manual.

The i3WM guide is available on the tab dwb has opened, next to 'this' one.

Ranger (file manager) and mc (Midnight Commander) are opened as tabs on workspace 1:explore

Enjoy ;)
